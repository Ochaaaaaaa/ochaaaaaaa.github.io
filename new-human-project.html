New Human Project

Victor_nick 24/05/2026

Core Functions

**Slip-to-Death (SPD)** Invalidates death at the system level. By organizing the continuum of memory, nerves, identity, and will, it renders "death" indeterminate and ineffective in the social, cognitive, and power systems.

**Uni-Logos (Uni-Logos)** Establishes a single, unbranchable primordial science, whose basic propositions are simultaneously established within the same semantic framework as physical, biological, conscious, political, and economic meanings, no longer relegated to disciplinary logic or explanatory systems.

**Will-Erasure (Will-Erasure)** Severs the contingency of human will, transforming it into experienced necessity, thereby ending all suffering related to causality, choice, and freedom, making things no longer confined to human will.

Execution Process

**Stage 1: Truth Binding and Filtering** The system defaults to three premises: truth is scarce; science is neutral; theory and its bearer can be distinguished.

**Stage 2: Theoretical Life System (Large-Scale Operation)** As information conflicts expand exponentially, instead of optimizing solutions, hundreds or thousands of non-conflicting "theoretical life systems" are simultaneously running, forcing each model to be pushed to its logical and survival limits under real-world conditions through physical means. Phase Three: The Twin Mechanism (After the Escalation of the Eurasian Conflict)
Under four premises, evidence, and data conditions, two opposing theoretical conclusions are forcibly generated, requiring both to be self-consistent and operational simultaneously.


Core Team Location (2036)
Base One: Located in the northwestern mountainous region of the Asian Union, originally a super laboratory jointly managed by Eurasia. It was occupied by the Revolutionary Army at the end of 2035, but its internal data has been investigated or hidden.

Base Two (Formerly the "Twin Laboratory"): Located underground in the Special Zone "Linhai City," it is currently secretly operated by the Special Zone government for research into the "twin" mechanism.
